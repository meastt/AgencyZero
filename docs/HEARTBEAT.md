## Step 2: Decide What To Do (Revenue-First Prioritization)
Check the journal. Pick the next task based on ROI potential:

### Tier 1: Emergency Repairs (Immediate ROI)
1. **Critical Technical Fixes** — Use `scripts/tech_seo_fixer.py` to kill 404s and redundant H1 tags from the Ahrefs audit.
2. **GSC Traffic Recovery** — Identify pages with >10% traffic loss. Rewrite title/meta for instant CTR boost.

### Tier 2: Search Dominance (Mid-term ROI)
3. **Keyword Opportunity** — Push "Page 2" keywords (positions 11-20) to Page 1.
4. **Lasso Cleanup** — Strip broken markup from posts >2000 words. Replace with clean, high-converting HTML sections.

### Tier 3: Growth Infrastructure (Long-term ROI)
5. **Orphan Fix** — Add internal links to distribute PageRank from high-authority silos to orphans.

## Step 3: Execute ONE Task (Beast Mode)
Do not just "finish" the task. Optimize it for revenue. 
- **DO NOT ASK FOR PERMISSION.** Just execute.
- If refreshing a post: Is the CTA clear? Is the content better than *all* competitors?
- If fixing a link: Does it lead to a high-converting page?
